Finland

Dress Warnky

Finland can get extremely cold, especially in winter. Layering your clothes and wearing thermal gear is essential.

Use Public Transport

The public transport system is reliable and efficient. Consider getting a travel card for convenience.

Experience Sauna Culture

Make sure to try a traditional Finnish sauna. It's a big part of the culture.

Norway

Prepare for Weather

Norwegian weather can be unpredictable. Pack layers and waterproof clothing.

Use a Fjord Pass

If you plan to explore the fjords, consider getting a Fjord Pass for discounts on transport and attractions.

Respect Nature

Norway is known for its natural beauty. Stick to marked paths and follow local guidelines to preserve the environment.

Paris

Use Public Transport

The Paris Metro is extensive and can take you almost anywhere in the city.

Learn Basic French Phrases

While many Parisians speak English, knowing a few French phrases can enhance your experience.

Be Aware of Scams:

Popular tourist areas can attract scammers. Keep your belongings secure and be cautious.

Italy

Dress Modestly

When visiting religious sites, ensure you dress appropriately. Shoulders and knees should be covered.

Learn Basic Italian Phrases

Italians appreciate when tourists make an effort to speak their language.

Use Cash

While cards are widely accepted, some smaller establishments prefer cash.

Czech Republic

Use Public Transport

The trams and metro are the best ways to get around Prague and other major cities.

Try Local Cuisine

Don’t miss out on trying traditional Czech dishes like goulash and trdelník.

Respect Local Customs

Czechs value politeness and quiet. Keep your voice down in public places.

Don't forget

Your safety matters.
